# ADHD Coaching Platform Concept

## Problem Statement

People with ADHD wait months for professional support while struggling daily with structure, focus, and follow-through across work, finances, and personal life — and generic self-help apps don't get how their brain works.

## Target Audience

**Primary:** ADHD-affected adults in the DACH region

**Segments:**
- Self-employed professionals
- Young professionals
- Anyone seeking structured daily support between therapy sessions

## Solution

A personal AI agent that acts as an always-available coaching companion, powered by real expert knowledge.

### Core Mechanics

#### Cassettes System
Verified ADHD coaches create and sell their methods as **"cassettes"** — plug-in knowledge packs that give the agent depth no generic chatbot can match.

- Coaches package their proven methodologies into reusable formats
- Users purchase and plug cassettes into their personal AI agent
- Each cassette adds specialized domain expertise (focus, finances, work structure, emotional regulation, etc.)

### Value Proposition

#### For Users
- **Affordable:** Accessible pricing compared to traditional coaching
- **Always Available:** 24/7 support when they need it most
- **Personalized:** Guidance tailored to their specific challenges and goals
- **Expert-Backed:** Real methodologies from verified ADHD coaches, not generic advice

#### For Coaches
- **Scalable Revenue:** Create once, sell infinitely
- **New Income Stream:** Monetize expertise without trading more hours for money
- **Reach:** Help more people than 1:1 sessions allow
- **Verified Status:** Platform endorsement builds trust and authority

## Key Differentiators

1. **Real Expert Knowledge** — Not generic AI responses, but proven coaching methodologies
2. **Plug-in Architecture** — Users customize their agent with specific expertise they need
3. **Coach Economy** — Incentivizes quality content creation from real professionals
4. **ADHD-Specific Design** — Built from ground up for how ADHD brains actually work
5. **DACH Region Focus** — Localized for German-speaking market with cultural context
